Case Study: "On Wednesday We Wear Black" GIF Design


This case study explores the GIF design titled "On Wednesday We Wear Black," created to promote the second season of the show Wednesday. The project began with an isometric design using Adobe Illustrator, where I aimed to recreate the iconic Addams Family home. After completing this initial phase, we moved on to phase two, which involved developing two additional pieces, culminating in an iterative three-part project for my final portfolio. The overall objective of this design was to achieve a high grade from my professor in my Illustrator 2 course, as this was the final assignment for the class. I am pleased to report that I achieved a 100% in all phases of these assignments.

Objectives

Visual Engagement: To create a visually captivating GIF that draws attention and embodies the show's dark, whimsical tone.

Brand Messaging: To effectively convey the show's theme and connect with its audience through recognizable imagery and phrases.

Promotion: To generate buzz and anticipation for the release of the second season.

Academic Achievement: To secure a good grade on the final assignment for my Illustrator 2 course.

Target Audience

Demographics: Primarily teens and young adults aged 13-30 who are fans of the series and enjoy supernatural or gothic themes.

Psychographics: Individuals who appreciate dark humor, quirky characters, and unique storytelling.

Design Elements

Color Palette

Dark Tones: The use of blacks and grays creates a moody atmosphere, aligning with the show's aesthetic.

Contrasting Highlights: Occasional lighter colors, such as the moon, add depth and draw attention to key elements.

Typography

Similar Font: The font used is reminiscent of the Wednesday TV show's title font, enhancing brand recognition.

Bold Presentation:The quote "We wear black on Wednesday," taken directly from the show, is presented in a striking, bold font that commands attention.

Imagery

Gothic ElementsThe haunted house and twisted trees evoke the show's eerie setting, immersing viewers in its world.

Animated Features: Subtle animations, like flickering lights or moving shadows, enhance the spooky ambiance and engage viewers.

Implementation

Platforms: The GIF is designed for use on social media, promotional websites, and streaming platforms to maximize reach.

Engagement Strategies: Encourage fans to share the GIF using a dedicated hashtag, creating a sense of community and excitement for the new season.

Results

The GIF design successfully added flair to promotional efforts on social media, generating buzz among fans of the series. Viewers responded positively, sharing their excitement for the upcoming season and engaging in conversations about the show's themes and characters. This positive reception also contributed to my goal of receiving a strong grade for the project, and I achieved a 100% in all phases of the assignments.
