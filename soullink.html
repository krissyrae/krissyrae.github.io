Case Study: "Soul Link" Invention Design


This case study focuses on the invention design titled "Soul Link," created as part of a project to develop a creative product that does not currently exist in the real world. The concept aims to bridge our world with the afterlife, providing a way for physical touch with lost loved ones. The project involved several phases: conceptualizing the invention, building the design using different images and masking tools in Photoshop, and finally creating an advertisement that would persuade potential customers to purchase it.

Objectives

Creative Innovation: To invent a unique product that provides a connection between the living and the deceased.

Visual Representation: To bring the invention to life using various design elements and techniques in Photoshop.

Marketing Strategy: To develop a compelling name and tagline for the product, along with an effective advertisement to attract potential customers.

Target Audience

Soul Link: A box designed with Ouija board elements, containing a sheet that can be possessed by a loved one upon summoning, allowing users to hug and physically connect with them once again.

Design Elements

Color Palette

Ethereal Colors: A palette of blues and greens to evoke a mystical and otherworldly feel, representing the connection to the afterlife.

Typography

Ouija Board Font: The text on the box was created using a font reminiscent of a Ouija board, enhancing the supernatural theme.

Carved Wood Text Style: The name "Soul Link" features a text style that resembles carved wood, adding a tactile quality to the design.

Imagery

Ghost Figure: A translucent ghost figure is depicted above the box, symbolizing the connection with the afterlife.

The Box: The design features a meticulously crafted box that serves as the focal point of the invention.

Homey Touch: A carpet underneath the box adds warmth and familiarity to the overall presentation.

Atmospheric Effects: Wispy brush strokes are used to create an ethereal atmosphere, enhancing the mystical quality of the design.

Tagline

Slogan: "Bridging Their World and Ours," effectively summarizes the purpose of the invention and invites emotional connection.

Engagement Strategies: Encourage fans to share the GIF using a dedicated hashtag, creating a sense of community and excitement for the new season.

Implementation

Digital Tools: Utilized Photoshop for masking and layering different images to create a cohesive and visually appealing design.

Advertising Strategy: Present the product in a way that emphasizes its emotional impact and the experience of reconnecting with loved ones.

Results

The "Soul Link" design successfully encapsulated the concept of bridging the living with the afterlife, generating an emotional response from viewers. The combination of creative visuals and a poignant tagline resonated with the intended audience, making the invention feel both relatable and intriguing.

Conclusion

The "Soul Link" invention design effectively combined innovative thinking with artistic execution, resulting in a compelling advertisement that successfully communicates the product's unique value. By achieving the objectives of creative innovation, visual representation, and marketing strategy, this project not only fulfilled the assignment requirements but also showcased my ability to conceptualize and present a novel idea. This approach to design can serve as a template for future projects that aim to blend creativity with emotional resonance.
